How I work

  1. A Python library called Mediapipe detects the coordinates of your joints in the video.
  2. A machine learning model uses those coordinates to detect if you are in push-up position.
  3. Other models then use those coordinates to look for imperfections in your form.
  4. All detections are recorded, and then a workout summary is generated.
